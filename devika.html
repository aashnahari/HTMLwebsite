Overview:

The Pug is a breed of dog originally from China, with physically distinctive features of a wrinkly, short-muzzled face and curled tail.

Characteristics:
  1. curled tail
  2. flat snout
  3. large brown eyes
  4. cute
  5. playful and loyal
  6. an ideal weight of 14-18 lbs


Fun facts:
  • They were bred to be lap dogs.
  • They are meant to have wrinkles.
  • favorites of the Chinese royals
  • they were a symbol for a secret society
  • they are one of the oldest dog breeds
